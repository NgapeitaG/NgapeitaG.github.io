Learning plan


My strengths are that:

• I work very hard and possess a great deal of stamina. I take immense satisfaction in being busy and productive.
• I look to the future and what possibilities there are.
• I enjoy close relationships with others. I find deep satisfaction in working hard with friends to achieve a goal.
• I enjoy routine and structure. My world is best described by the order I create. I like my life to be neat and tidy in other words.
• I take psychological ownership of what I say I will do. I’m committed to stable values such as honesty and loyalty.

My Limitations are that:

• I like consistency and crave stable routines and clear rules and procedures that everyone can follow.
• I can procrastinate sometimes and often get easily distracted, but when I’m really into something, I will commit to the task.
• Sometimes I’m not good at putting my thoughts into words at first.
• I need to be a more analytical person and really study a problem to figure out the solution.

To manage my workload, I will:

• commit my time
• Work very hard
• Have a positive and motivated attitude towards any tasks which are given to me
• Don’t leave tasks to the last minute
• Break my project into sub-tasks to make the one project more easier to complete in steps
• Not to waste time on watching youtube
