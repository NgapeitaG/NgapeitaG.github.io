Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

When people understand the basic principles of neuroplasticity, they change the way their brain is wired and allow their brains the ability to grow. Neuroplasticity is around the growth of the brain. When we have what we call a ‘fixed mindset’, we simply give up so to speak and don’t try to open our minds. When you apply the principles of neuroplasticity, we have the ability to modify our brains connections so that we can rewire it to think in a different way. We are essentially expanding our minds and changing the way we think. One principle of neuroplasticity is going over something new which you’ve learnt repeatedly. The more you practice something, the stronger the connections will be in your brain, ensuring that you retain that information for the future.

What are some of the ways to increase your Neuroplasticity?

  1. Cause some mild stress to brain and force it to restructure its neuron connections.
  2. Whole brain holistic thinking
  3. Practice your motor skills – e.g. Brushing your teeth with your non-dominant hand
  4. Practice flow – It’s the zone that puts us into tunnel vision into our creative genius. There needs a be a balance between the challenge and your skills.
  5. Learn a new skills which doesn’t take a lot of time but forces you to use your mental muscles. Read new books about topics you don’t have the slightest knowledge about.
  6. Shake up your daily routine and mix it up a little bit.
  7. Meditation – Increase attention span, improved memory. Increases the coherence of the different hemispheres of the brain. Is a great stress reliever.
  8. Intermittent fasting – Great way to increase BDNF levels and has many other health benefits.
  9. Strip up old patterns and create new ones

Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

A growth mindset is wiring your brain to solve difficult problems, learn new things and essentially to think growth. It is very relevant because we as a society have wired ourselves to have to be rewarded in order to submit and work for something. We are addicted to instant gratification and like to be praised for our accomplishments. A growth mindset will increase your intelligence, how much you learn and how you allow your mind to expand. You move out of a fixed mindset and turn your brain into a brain which wants to learn and try new things. This is a growth mindset. A mindset where you utilize your brains capabilities and set it into growth mode.
